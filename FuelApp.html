Fuel Calculator for Ride Sharers

This application is still a concept. After rises in demand for the application Eskom se Push and noticing that a lot of people are ride sharing due to high petrol costs, I began to think of an application that would allow users to easily track the amount of fuel they have used in ride sharing and then request payment (or make payment) to the driver of the car.
